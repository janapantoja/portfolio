DASHBOARD

Challenge

Contractors at the company help researchers around the world succeed by translating academic papers. Managers and contractors work remotely, and the online dashboard is used to perform critical tasks. Managers indicate that an excessive amount of time is spent on training contractors to use the dashboard. Also, as translators tried to work with the platform, they encounter many critical errors. Managers would like to revamp their website.

My Role

Strategy, research and design

Deliverables

User survey, user interviews, heuristic evaluation, personas, wireframes, prototype
