SCIENCE WORKFORCE

Challenge

Contractors at the company helped researchers around the world succeed by providing academic services. Managers and contractors worked remotely, and the online platform was used to perform critical tasks. Managers indicated that an excessive amount of time is spent on training contractors to use the platform. Managers would like to revamp their website.

My Role

Strategy, research and design

Deliverables

User survey, user interviews, heuristic evaluation, personas, wireframes, prototype

Main tools

Googles forms, NVivo, Aabel, and OmniGraffle

Process

Step #2: Analyzing user surveys

Next, I analyzed past satisfaction surveys using Aabel statistical analysis software. Also, I used NVivo to produce a word cloud as a tool for preliminary analysis of the survey responses provided by contractors.The word assignments was frequently used in the surveys. After generating a word tree, the results indicated problems finding and claiming papers on the website.

Step #3: Interviewing users

I conducted remote interviews with 12 users (contractors and managers). These interviews revealed that the main source of frustration was the need of help for completion of online tasks.

Step #4: Understanding how users complete tasks

I investigated the sequence that experienced contractors used to perform on the assignment page. This user flow shows the steps taken, helping us identify which ones fail most often and better understanding the user needs.
