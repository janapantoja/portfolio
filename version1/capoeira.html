CAPOEIRA

Challenge

Capoeira is a martial art. People who practice it travel all over the world for events where they meet new people, take special workshops and develop their skills. During these trips, capoeiristas live and breath capoeira 24/7, and the event organizers are responsible for coordinating housing, transportation, training schedule, performances etc. This app was designed to help facilitate the logistics of Capoeira events.
