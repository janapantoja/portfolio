BRAZILIAN STORE

Challenge

The owners of a Brazilian grocery store are interested in leveraging ecommerce to maximize their sales. To increase engagemente, they want to offer a space for users to share their recipes and buy ingredients online.

My Role

Research and design

Deliverables

Concept mapping, user interviews, personas, paper prototype, mockups

Main tools

POP, Keynote, and Trello

Process

Step #1: Stakeholder meeting

Met with major stakeholders: couple of owners and the store manager.

Step #2: Concept mapping

Organized ideas from stakeholders interviews and created a concept map, which allowed me to better understand stakeholders expectations.

Step #3: User Interviews

I reached out to returning clients as well as potential customers who live far from the store's location. My goal was to learn how they shop and how an app would help them with their groceries.The users' main goals were the following: 1. users require a safe transaction method if they were to use the app. Many of them don't feel safe purchasing groceries online but think it would save them time; 2. they would like the option to communicate with the store during shopping; 3. users would like access to recipes as they shop or add products to their wish lists.
